# CV Generation Preferences

Use this file to describe how you want your 1-page and 4-page CVs to be generated. 

### Workflow Options
1. **Natural Language (AI-Assisted)**: Write your preferences here and tell your AI assistant to "Update the config."
2. **Pinpoint Control (Autonomous)**: Add `version: 1-Page` or `version: 4-Page` directly to an entry in your data YAML files (e.g., `publications.yml`).
3. **Structured Control (Autonomous)**: Edit `cv_config.yml` directly to change entry limits or toggle entire sections.
